LinkedIn's Algorithm

  • Initial Filter: a bot determines if your post is spam, low-quality, or clear
  • Testing: your post is shown to a small group of people to see if people 'hide' your content or 'Report as Spam'
  • Content Scoring: your post is scored based on likes, comments, and shares
  • Real People Assessment: if your post has high engagement, it will be viewed by actual people at LinkedIn to decide whether to share your post outside your network/on 'trending content'

Hiring and Getting Hired

  • Employees sourced through LinkedIn are 40% less likely to leave the company within the first 6 months
  • Job seekers with a comprehensive LinkedIn profile have a 71% higher chance of getting an interview
  • 87% of recruiters find LinkedIn to be the most effective way to vet candidates during the hiring process
  • Confirmed hires starting from LinkedIn increased 22% in 2022
